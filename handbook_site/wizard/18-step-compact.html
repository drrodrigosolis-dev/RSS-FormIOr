Page 18: Step 9 - Compact Multi-Select Columns

Reduce repeated selection fields into a single compact column to simplify analysis.

What you will be asked

You will see a short prompt with a recommended default. Press Enter to accept the default or provide a new value if you need to change it.

Why this step matters

This step keeps your workflow consistent and makes the output easier to share and reproduce across runs.

Tips

  • Best for long multi-select lists
  • Creates readable output in exports
  • Keeps original columns available if needed
