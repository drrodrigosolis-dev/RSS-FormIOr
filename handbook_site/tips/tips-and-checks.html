Tips and Quality Checks

This page lists practical checks you can apply to ensure your data and outputs are reliable.

Data quality checks

  • Confirm submission ID is unique after deduplication
  • Check missingness for key fields
  • Scan for unexpected option values
  • Confirm timestamps are within expected ranges

Export checks

  • Ensure the output file exists where expected
  • Verify row counts match expectations
  • Confirm the raw flattened sheet is included

Audit log checks

  • Confirm each major step produced a log entry
  • Review notes for warnings or skipped steps
